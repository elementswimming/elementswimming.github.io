About

Element Swimming was founded by Dominik Vavrečka and has been pushing the boundaries of Czech training since 2019.

Dominik is an athlete through and through. He applies the experience gained during his competitive career at the Ostrava Swimming Sports Club and his studies in Brno to his daily work with athletes. He is a multiple medalist at the Czech National Championships and a participant in European and World Championships. He currently works as a coach at the University Sports Centre Victoria.

His goal is to share up-to-date knowledge and help everyone develop their potential. This is how Element Swimming was created.
