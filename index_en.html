About

Dominik is an athlete in body and soul and sport is a part of him. He was a member of the Ostrava Swimming Club, where he was active for 10 years of his competitive career. The change came with his entry to Masaryk University in Brno. Here he continued his swimming career, throughout his studies. Within 5 years he managed to complete a Bachelor's degree in Coaching and a Master's degree in Conditioning Coach. During his sporting career Dominik has achieved several successes. He is a multiple medalist of the Czech Championship, a participant of the European Championship and World Championships. Now Dominik works as a coach at the Prague University Sports Centre known as Victoria.

Dominic is driven forward by the knowledge from his sporting and coaching career. He wants to pass them on and help develop the potential of his charges. It is thanks to all these suggestions and experiences that the unique Element swimming project was created.

Services

At Element Swimming we believe that individual lessons are the key to achieving your swimming goals. That's why we only work with certified coaches from the Czech Swimming Federation Swimming Sports Association. Each of our coaches has undergone rigorous swim training, allowing them to bring you not only experience, but also a passion for swimming.

Our trainers are top in their field due to their dedication and qualifications. With us, it's not just about training, it's also about passing on knowledge to our clients. We are proud to be able to offer our swimming services not only in Prague, Brno, Ostrava and Krnov, but also through Online coaching to make us as accessible as possible to you.

We would love to help you achieve your swimming goals. For more information about our services and prices in a specific city, feel free to click on your city in our price list.

Cookie Policy

This Cookies and Similar Technologies Policy applies to the www.elementswimming.cz website.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored by your browser on your computer or device when you visit a website. Cookies allow the website to recognize the device and remember certain information about your browsing sessions during the connection. Cookies remember the type of browser used or settings, such as language, which remain the default setting upon repeated visits to the website. The data collected by cookies is aggregated and anonymous.

What do we use cookies for?

Performance cookies allow us to analyze how you interact with our website. They can be used to improve and simplify the usability of our web pages. The data collected by these cookies does not personally identify you.

The cookies we use do not harm your computer and cannot be used to collect your personal data.

We use a third-party provider, Google Analytics, or a link to this service on our website. These links (or any other content from third-party providers) may contain third-party cookies, and therefore, you should read the information on the use of such cookies in the relevant third party's cookie policy.

How can I control or delete cookies?

Most browsers accept cookies automatically. If you do not want to use cookies, you will need to actively delete them or block them. If you refuse the use of cookies, you will still be able to visit our website, but some features may not work correctly. If you wish to do so, you can choose to refuse or block cookies by changing the settings on your internet browser. For more information, please use your browser help.
